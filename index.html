Feel · Breath · Explore

Located between the forested mountains of Champagne-Ardennes, Hilltop B&B is the place where you disconnect from your daily routine and really relax. Whether you come for a day in the middle of nature, for a weekend getaway or a family vacation, at Hilltop B&B you will always have a personalized experience and you will spend memorable moments.

Our accomodation

Our House has nine rooms, all different, which have been thought out down to the smallest detail: combination of wood and slate, sanded beams, high-end bedding, natural linen bed linen from Couleur Chanvre, private terraces for some.

Without forgetting, our independent cottage, La Bergerie, which has a warm decoration, with a panoramic view of the sea.
