Outdoor Activities

Spending time outside is a fun option

Hiking

If you’re an adventurer who loves being out in nature, a hiking tour should be on your list. You can expect great wildlife photography opportunities in Butte de vauqois. In addition, hiking will give you the opportunity to clear your mind and escape regular activities that fill your day.

Cycling

Why not cycle around and find some nice, hidden places? Maybe something new to you could be hiding out under a big, old tree. You could even get off your bike and check out those spots to see how comfortable they’d be.

Sightseeing

Historical place

Montfaucon American Monument

The World War I Montfaucon American Monument is located seven miles south of the Meuse-Argonne American Cemetery and Memorial and 20 miles northwest of Verdun, France.
