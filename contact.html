We are

Johanna

As a little girl, Hilltop B&B meant freedom to me. I used to live in Paris with my family, and we came as often as possible to visit my grandmother. She hosted the bread and breakfast at that time, when i was there i had the chance to learn from her how to deal with the guests, how to make them feel home and relaxed. When she passed away, i was the one who learned everything from her, so I am here to host your stay at this beautiful place.

&

Sebastian

When I met Johanna in Paris, I was already frustrated with the big city life, her stories from this countryside heaven were the things that kept the hope alive in me. It was clear from the beginning that we would finally get away from the noise and settle down in this peace of heaven. I still have a lot to learn from her, but i can only promise you the best.
