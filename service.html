Special Services

Hilltop is easily accessible for people in wheelchairs. First and foremost, you will find a ramp at the entrance of the property. Also, to reach the second floor you might use the lift to get there. But all necessary facilities are placed downstairs such as the bedroom, bathroom, and the kitchen. The wide corridors and doorways will make it easy to maneuver through the house with a wheelchair.
In the bathrooms you will find handrails on the walls and a roll-in shower.
If there are any food requests (vegan, allergies, halal,...) please let us know beforehand so we can take this into account.
